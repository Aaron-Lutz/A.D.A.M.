Who is A.D.A.M.?

A.D.A.M. (Automated Digital Assistant Mainframe) represents what is possible with AI assistance technology. Currently in pre-alpha testing, this project aims to seamlessly integrate state-of-the-art AI with Google Workspace, advanced search capabilities, and modern communication platforms. While still in early development, A.D.A.M. already demonstrates remarkable capabilities in task automation, enhanced by natural voice interactions and a uniquely engaging personality.

Google Workspace Integration

Seamlessly automate your daily workflow across Gmail, Calendar, Tasks, Contacts, and Drive.

Experience next-level productivity with A.D.A.M.'s deep integration into Google Workspace:

  • Retrieve, send or delete emails including attachments
  • Retrieve, edit, create or delete events including participant invitations
  • Retrieve, edit, create or delete tasks
  • Retrieve, edit, create and delete contacts. Also used to automatically rertrieve contact details when you need to reach out to someone
  • Upload, download, move, rename or delete your files and folders in Drive

Intelligent Information Access

Get instant access to weather updates, news, and powerful web search capabilities.

Stay informed and up-to-date with A.D.A.M.'s comprehensive information services:

  • Personalized news curation based on your interests
  • Advanced web search with context awareness
  • Information synthesis from multiple sources
  • Real-time weather forecasts and alerts

Enhanced Communication

Manage calls and messages across platforms with unprecedented ease.

Transform your communication experience with A.D.A.M.'s integrated features:

  • Seamless call placing and scheduling. Uses websearch, contacts or context to find the phone number to call.
  • WhatsApp message handling from you own WhatsApp account

Natural Voice Interaction

Engage in fluid, natural conversations with just a "Hey, Adam" command.

Experience the future of AI interaction with A.D.A.M.'s voice capabilities:

  • Natural language processing for human-like conversations.
  • Real-time speech-to-speech responses
  • Responds to emotions and intonation and can adapt the voice on the fly.
  • Multi-turn conversation handling. Interrupt at any time.

Computer Use (coming soon)

Let A.D.A.M. control a virtual computer for complex tasks.

Give A.D.A.M. a virtual Computer to use to solve longer and more complex tasks that are not integrated by default.:

  • Control the Mouse and Keyboard
  • Access the terminal
  • Can do practically anyhting a human can at a computer to solve your edge use cases.
  • Keep everything secure by running it on a virtual machine.
